# Meeting information

A directory for managing agendas and keeping minutes of WG/CG teleconferences. 
See [directories at the top](../) for face-to-face meetings.

## Teleconferences

This immersive web working group (WG) and community group (CG) hold 
bi-weekly teleconferences to discuss work items, teleconferenes are 
held by tunrs and weekly in total for WG and CG.

Meeting agendas are published before each teleconference within directory per 
each year (such as [2026](2026/)), and public-immersive-web (CG) or public-immersive-web-wg (WG) mailing list 
(archive: [CG](https://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/public-immersive-web/), 
[WG](https://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/public-immersive-web-wg/)), 
following [instructions](https://github.com/immersive-web/administrivia#agenda-creation-instructions-for-teleconferences). 
W3C calendar ([WG](https://www.w3.org/groups/wg/immersive-web/calendar/), 
[CG](https://www.w3.org/groups/cg/immersive-web/calendar/)) include information 
about how to join a call, and how to connect to IRC (irc.w3.org:6667 #immersive-web, 
also [webirc](https://webirc.w3.org/#/connect?channels=immersive-web) is available) and 
[instructions](https://github.com/immersive-web/administrivia/blob/main/IRC.md), 
which is used during meetings to record minutes and also for speaker queue 
management. 

Minutes and summaries of all teleconferences can be found in each agenda page.
Also you can find from [meeting minutes browser](https://www.w3.org/services/meeting-minutes/immersive-web/?num=25).
